Who am I?

Ever since I was young I have been interested in computers and how they work. As I continued to grow, so did my interest in computing. After making simple programs and building my own computer in highschool, I knew that computing was my passion and that I wanted to pursue a career in computing. When I graduated high school, I wanted to learn how to grow as a developer, so I applied to Rochester Institute of Technology. By spring of my second year in college, I decided that I wanted to focus on making software that can help people on the go. As of now, I have recently graduated from RIT with a degree in Web and Mobile Development. At the moment, I am looking for a junior level developer position that allows me to showcase what I learned so far in addition to furthering my skills in a real world environment.
